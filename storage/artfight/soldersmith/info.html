Lore

Was originally human, or, was designed to replace said human, by said human.

The original Carter Soldersmith (Human) is embedded in the robot as memories and as the DNA template used for the artificial skin their creation wears today.

The first version completely replaced Carter, being a gutted version of himself with his memories, essentially. The robot - or Carter as of then, essentially having turned himself into the robot as the memory data - then aimed to upgrade itself to be what Carter couldn't.

The reason for this procedure had not been extracted from Carter while he was alive, as a human, although some speculate it had to do with his grandmother's death.
