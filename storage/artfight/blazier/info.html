Lore

He was originally a blaze as most are, but along his journey he was befriended by a human who could upgrade his form by encasing it to harness and control it's power.

Thus, a Blaze Burner was made.

The burner was then placed upon a humanoid rig, in an effort to make the Blaze feel more welcomed, being treated as an equal rather than a monster of sort.
